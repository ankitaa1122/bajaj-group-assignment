Rooted in Freedom,
Driven by Innovation

The Bajaj Group, founded by freedom fighter and philanthropist Jamnalal Bajaj, straddles Sugar, Ethanol, Power, and FMCG businesses. Established during India's fight for independence, the group blends business success with a strong commitment to social responsibility.

Built on Self‑Reliance,
Guided by Purpose

Inspired by Mahatma Gandhi's vision of self‑sufficiency, Hindustan Sugar Mills Ltd was launched. This was one of the 30 pioneering mills that laid the foundation for India’s sugar industry. Today, Bajaj Hindusthan Sugar Ltd. stands as India’s top sugar producer and a global industry leader.

Fueled by Patriotism,
Produced as Necessity

The group's distillery unit began production, pioneering alcohol‑mixed petrol supply to the army during World War II. We are now one of India's largest Ethanol producers fostering green fuel usage and reducing carbon emissions.

One of World’s Largest
Sugar Companies

Bajaj Hindusthan Sugar has been at the forefront of India’s journey towards self-sufficiency in sugar production. As a pioneer of the country’s fuel ethanol program and one of the world’s largest sugar companies, we have consistently set new benchmarks in the industry.

Embracing Green Ethanol
for a Sustainable Future

Bajaj Hindusthan Sugar (BHSL) is a leading producer of green ethanol, playing a crucial role in the renewable energy landscape. Derived from sugarcane molasses, our clean and renewable ethanol significantly reduces carbon emissions and lessens dependence on fossil fuels. Our zero-waste production process maximises efficiency by utilising every part of the sugarcane, transforming molasses—a by-product of sugar manufacturing—into ethanol without waste.

This commitment to sustainability not only fuels vehicles but also propels progress. Together, let’s embrace a greener tomorrow and make a lasting impact on our planet through sustainable energy.
